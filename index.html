Consultation checklist

Questions to ask

  • Shoe purpose
  • Aches and pains
  • Current shoe used for this activity
  • Size they're used to
  • Diabetes — if so, neuropathy?
  • Is anyone else being fit today?
  • Doctor-recommended shoe or inserts?
  • What they like/dislike about current shoe
  • Color restrictions (e.g. for work)
  • Goals for the shoe (balance, speed, training, etc.)
  • How often they run / what they're training for
  • Sock height, cushion, color, snugness
  • Cross-sell: nutrition fuel or blister products?

Things to notice

  • Swelling
  • Bruises, bunions, blisters, hammer toes
  • Toe fungus
  • When the last scan was done
  • Socks currently wearing
  • Colors they're wearing / color of shoe
  • Balance
  • Age

Scan steps

  • Explain the scan ahead of time — be exact in directions
  • Socks and shoes off, both feet; roll pants to knee
  • Oofos on to walk to scan, off for the clean scan
  • Stand in the box — don't cover circles or lines, stand still
  • Cross the pressure plate: walk past the blue tape, straight across, looking up, naturally — repeat a few times
  • Oofos back on, head back to bench, explain the scan data
